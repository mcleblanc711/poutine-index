About the Poutine Index

Inspired by The Economist's Big Mac Index, the Poutine Index tracks the price of classic poutine (fries, cheese curds, and gravy) across Canadian cities. We also calculate an "affordability index" showing how many minutes of minimum wage work it takes to buy a poutine.

Click on any city marker to see average prices for small, regular, and large portions, along with the local affordability index and the number of restaurants sampled.
